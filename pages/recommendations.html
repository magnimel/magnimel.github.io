MY FAVORITE ANIME


Steins;Gate

Strange, but amazing Sci-Fi/thriller about a self-proclaimed mad scientist with time-traveling abilities. Steins;Gate's premise centers on people messing with time travel, but as the plot develops, depth and conspiracies are fed to you.

See on Crunchroll
Dr. Stone

The exceptionally intelligent and science-driven boy Senku Ishigami awakens several thousand years after a mysterious phenomenon turns all of humanity to stone. This show is essentially science on steroids if you already love science.

See on Crunchroll
Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation

This show is about a 34-year-old jobless, worthless, and unattractive lowlife who died and was reincarnated in a fantasy world. He retains his previous life's memories and trauma. People don't change so easily, but this time, he resolved to get serious about living.

See on Crunchroll
Re:Zero − Starting Life in Another World

Here, a 17-year-old shut-in gets transported into a fantasy world. He is excited about the situation, but of course he is still the SAME worthless shut-in. So he quickly died. But he can reset his life to a point in time every time he dies. So, in this unforgiving and dark world, he must struggle (and die a lot) to survive.
